About the Tradition

Linéum is more than just a pen — it's a story engraved in time. Each student receives a personalized, refillable pen engraved with their name and the year, creating a lasting memory.

Refill System

Dedicated refill machines in school allow students to refill their Linéum pens using coins or credit cards, supporting sustainability and reuse.

Auction & Charity

Students who’ve kept their Linéum pen for over 2 years can auction them at annual school events, with all proceeds going to the Steering Committee or a selected charity.

Vision 2030

In Le Rosey’s 150th anniversary in 2030, the oldest surviving Linéum pens will be honored and auctioned in a special ceremony.
